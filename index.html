Holiday Rejection Sampler

Happy Thanksgiving, ChIDEO! Celebrate with Pi!

Ingredients

  • A square with side length of 1
    • A circle with radius of 1 A uniform random number generator on [0, 1]

Directions

  • 1. Inscribe the unit circle within the unit square, placing its center at one of the square's corners
    • 2. Generate two random numbers, forming a coordinate pair. Plot it within your square, noting whether or not it also falls within the circle. 3. Repeat step 2 to taste, tracking both \(C =\) number of points in the circle and \(N =\) total number of points sampled, until your approximation is golden brown. 4. As \(N \to \infty\), \({C \over N} \to {{\pi r^2 \over 4} \div r^2}\), the area of the circle divided by the area of the square. Cancelling \(r^2\), we see that \({C \over N} \to {\pi \over 4}\). As such, \(4 * {C \over N} \to \pi\). 5. Serve at localhost:3000
